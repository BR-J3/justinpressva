About

Justin Press VA is independent accountability journalism covering Southside Virginia — Henry County, Martinsville, and the region's public institutions.

The work is simple: find what the public record shows, document it accurately, and publish it without spin. Governance, public spending, civic accountability. No advertising. No access journalism. No agenda beyond the record itself.
